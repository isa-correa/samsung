The Friction Points
02 / 07
Visual Noise
Large data grids create an overwhelming amount of visual noise. Users seeking a specific quick answer are forced to process irrelevant information, slowing down their journey.
Hard to Scan
Users naturally scan web pages, but dense grids resist scanning. Finding the intersection of a specific feature and a specific phone model requires high cognitive effort.
Comparison Friction
Comparing the nuanced differences between the S25, S25+, and S25 Ultra becomes tedious when the data is not staged or prioritized logically for the user.
The Opportunity
03 / 07
From Static to Seamless
Transforming static information into an interactive exploration fundamentally improves both clarity and usability.
By moving from passive reading to active exploration, we can serve users exactly what they need, exactly when they ask for it.
This approach eliminates friction and delivers a premium, engaging user experience that aligns perfectly with the innovative Samsung Galaxy brand.

Proposed Solution
04 / 07
An interactive prototype where users select models and see information dynamically.
Visual Model Selection

Dynamic Information Display

Focused Comparison
Information is displayed more clearly, reducing visual clutter and making model differences easier to understand.
